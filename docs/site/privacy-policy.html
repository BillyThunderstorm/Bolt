Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

About This Application

Foxy is a personal streaming assistant tool created and operated solely by the individual developer for their own personal use. It is not a commercial product and is not available to the general public.

Data Collection

Foxy does not collect, store, or share personal data from any third-party users. The application is used exclusively by its developer to manage their own streaming content on platforms including TikTok, Twitch, and related services.

TikTok Data Usage

Foxy accesses the TikTok API solely to:

  • Upload video content created by the developer to the developer's own TikTok account
  • Read basic account information for the developer's own account
  • Manage post scheduling for content the developer has personally created

No TikTok data is stored beyond what is necessary for the immediate operation of the tool. No data is shared with any third parties.

Data Storage

Any data temporarily stored by this application is stored locally on the developer's personal computer and is not transmitted to any external servers beyond the official APIs of the platforms being accessed (TikTok, Twitch, etc.).

Third-Party Services

This application interacts with the following third-party platforms using their official APIs and in accordance with their respective terms of service:

  • TikTok (content publishing)
  • Twitch (stream monitoring)
  • Streamlabs (stream alerts)
  • OBS Studio (local broadcast software)
