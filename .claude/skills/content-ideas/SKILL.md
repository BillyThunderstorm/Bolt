# Content Ideas
Generate specific, actionable content ideas for YouTube videos, Twitch streams, Amazon product reviews, and tech reviews. Tailor ideas to what's relevant in gaming, tech, and creator culture — not generic suggestions.
## How to Run an Ideas Session
When the user asks for ideas:
1. Ask for a focus area if they haven't given one: game, product category, niche topic, or platform. One question max.
2. Generate at least 10 ideas across relevant content types.
3. For each idea, include: the title/hook, the format (video, stream, review, short), and a one-line reason why it works.
4. Flag 2-3 as "top picks" with a brief note on why they'd perform.
## Idea Output Format
For each idea, present:
```
[TITLE / HOOK]
Format: YouTube Long-form | YouTube Short | TikTok | Twitch Stream | Amazon Review | Tech Review
Why it works: [one sentence]
```
## Content Pillars & Idea Types
**Gaming / Twitch**
- "I tried [game mechanic] for 24 hours" experiments
- Beginner vs. pro breakdowns of a game or genre
- "Why [popular game] is losing players" analysis
- Stream series: recurring formats (challenge runs, viewer games, learning a new game live)
- Reaction to new game trailers or announcements
- "Best games under $20" style roundups
**Tech Product Reviews**
- "$X vs $X+50: Is the upgrade worth it?" comparison style
- "I used [product] every day for 30 days" longevity test
- "Things nobody tells you about [product]" — hidden features or flaws
- "Best [category] for streamers / content creators on a budget"
- Unboxing + first impression + 30-day follow-up series
**Amazon Product Reviews**
- "Is it actually worth it?" verdict-style reviews
- "Amazon's #1 best seller in [category] — tested and ranked" roundups
- "I bought 5 cheap [products] so you don't have to" value tests
- Focus on: does it do what it claims? Who is it actually for?
**Social / Short-Form**
- React to trends in your niche with your own take
- "Hot take: [opinion]" style posts
- Before/after: showing growth, skill, or a setup improvement
- "Day in the life of a solo content creator" type content
## Hook Formulas That Work
Pull from these when generating titles:
- "I [did extreme thing] for [time period] — here's what happened"
- "Why [popular thing] is [surprising opinion]"
- "[Number] things I wish I knew before [experience]"
- "The truth about [product/game/trend]"
- "[Budget] vs [Premium]: I tested both so you don't have to"
- "Stop [common mistake] — do this instead"
## Trend Awareness
When brainstorming, consider:
- What's currently being released or talked about in gaming/tech
- Seasonal events (new game releases, holiday shopping, streaming milestones)
- What questions viewers are asking in the comments of similar creators
- Content gaps: what are popular creators in this niche NOT covering?
See `references/niche-research.md` for strategies to find trending topics.
