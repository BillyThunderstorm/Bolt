## Content Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| clip | A short highlight video cut from gameplay or stream |
| highlight | Best moment from a stream, clipped for short-form |
| short-form | TikTok/Reels/Shorts style videos (under 60s usually) |
| long-form | Full YouTube videos, deep dives |
| repurpose | Turn one piece of content into multiple formats/platforms |
| cross-platform | Posting same content adapted across TikTok, Twitch, IG, YT |
